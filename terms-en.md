## § 3 Description of the Service

### 3.1 What MindBalance does

MindBalance is a wellness and self-help app that supports users in coping with stress and preventing burnout. The App provides in particular:

- **Mood tracking:** daily mood check-ins with optional emotion tags
- **Balance Score:** algorithmically calculated score based on your entries
- **Burnout check (MBI):** weekly self-assessment based on the Maslach Burnout Inventory
- **Micro-interventions:** short breathing, reflection, and mindfulness exercises
- **AI coach Lumi:** text-based conversation with an AI coach (only with active consent, see § 12)
- **Emergency resources:** always one tap away — links to country-specific crisis hotlines (see § 4)
- **Daily and weekly insights:** automatically generated observations on your patterns
- **Wearable integration** (optional, Premium only): connection via Health Connect
- **Journaling, pattern detection, additional Premium features:** see § 6.2 for the full feature list

A complete and current feature list is available in the App's Google Play Store listing.

### 3.2 What MindBalance explicitly does not do

MindBalance is **not** a medical device under the EU Medical Device Regulation (MDR), **not** diagnostic software, and **not** a digital health application (DiGA) on prescription. MindBalance does not replace, in particular:

- treatment by a doctor, psychotherapist, or other qualified medical professional
- a medical diagnosis
- professional psychological, psychiatric, or psychotherapeutic counselling
- emergency or crisis care

The Provider makes **no** claim that MindBalance leads to measurable improvements in symptoms, mood, or quality of life for any individual user. The App offers self-reflection tools; any effect depends on individual factors that the Provider cannot control.

### 3.3 AI-based functions

Certain App functions (in particular the AI coach Lumi and daily insights) are based on Large Language Models ("LLMs") provided by the U.S. company Anthropic. AI-generated answers may be factually wrong, incomplete, or misleading. No warranty is given regarding the correctness, completeness, or fitness of AI answers for any specific purpose. Further notes on AI functions and data transmission to Anthropic: see § 12 and the [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy-en.md), § 2.2.

AI functions are **off** by default. Only after explicit consent via the corresponding toggle in `Profile → Data protection settings` is data transmitted to Anthropic. You may withdraw consent there at any time; with the withdrawal, data belonging to the AI coach (in particular chat history) is also removed from your device.

### 12.3 Limitations of AI Answers

Lumi is designed as a warm, empathic companion, **not** as a therapist, doctor, or diagnostician. Lumi gives no medical recommendations, no diagnoses, and no healing promises. AI answers may be wrong or misleading; **never rely on Lumi alone** for decisions with health consequences.

In crisis situations (e.g. expression of suicidal thoughts, self- or third-party endangerment), Lumi terminates the conversation and directs you to the emergency resources (see § 4).
